Naija Jollof

Spicy and tasty Nigerian rice cooked with tomatoes and pepper

Recipe Information

Preparation time : 20 minutes

Cooking time : 40 minutes

Number of Servings : 4 persons

Difficulty Level : Intermediate

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of rice
  • Good amount of Vegetable oil
  • 1 large onions chopped
  • 2 red bell pepper blended
  • 1 big tomato blended or cup of tomato puree
  • 2 cubes of Maggi
  • Salt
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 teaspon curry powder
  • 2 bay leaves

Cooking Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable oil and add the chopped onions till they are soft
  2. Add enough water into the pot alongside the blended pepper and tomatoes and let it cook for about 4-5 minutes
  3. Add the thyme, curry powder, maggi and bay leaves
  4. Rinse the rice and pour into the sauce and stir making sure each grain is coated in the sauce
  5. Leave to cook for about 30 minutes under supervision and check if the grains are soft for consumption
  6. Serve hot with any other condiments of your choice(chicken, beef, shrimp) and chilled drink

Note :Make sure the heat isn't too high
